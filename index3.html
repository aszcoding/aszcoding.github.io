Meet Our Team

Our well seasoned employees have over 50 years of combined experience in providing
exceptional care for dogs. All of our staff are certified via Red Cross in pet CPR and first aid,
and they're certified Professional Pet Sitters through the National Assocation of
Professional Pet Sitters (NAPPS). At The Puppy Play Place, you can take comfort
in knowing your pups are in excellent hands.

Carrie

Carrie turned her lifelong dream into a reality when she opened The Puppy Play Place in 2015. She ran this business on her own for the first year, and now she has plenty of helping hands. Carrie's dog's name is Ginger, and the two of them are inseparable!

Megan

Megan was the first person Carrie hired! She has been with us for 7 years now, and her favorite activity is training dogs on our agility course. Her dog, Sage, holds the record for the fastest completion of the course! Megan hopes to open a doggy daycare of her own one day.

Jennifer

Jennifer joined our team 2 years ago, and she primarily runs the daycare services. Jennifer and her dog Scout are big outdoor buffs! They love to hike on the weekends, and sometimes they'll even go for a swim!

James

James is our photographer and head dog trainer. He has been training dogs professionally for 8 years, and his French Bulldog, Max, is a certified Canine Good Citizen.

Nikki

Since the day she was born, Nikki has had a dog in her family. She is the newest addition to our team, but she has over 5 years of experience dog sitting on her own via Rover. She is extremely creative and is always making new enrichment activities for the dogs.

Laura

Laura is an amazing dog groomer! In fact, she is a National Certified Master Groomer! There's no dog too unkept for Laura! Dot, her Yorkshire Terrier, inspired her to start grooming dogs. The rest is history!
