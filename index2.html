Services

*For Daycayre & Boarding, dogs must:

Be spayed/neutered (if matured)
Be current on all vaccinations (Distemper, Bordetella, Rabies)
Pass a temperment test with one of our team members and another dog

*For Training & Grooming, dogs must:

Be current on all vaccinations (Distemper, Bordetella, Rabies)

Daycare

During daycare, we group dogs according to size and temperament. We offer several different activities throughout the day, such as treat puzzles, an agility course, water sprinklers, and a 3 foot deep rec pool that has a lifeguard present any time the dogs are around. Each dog will be taken on a walk twice a day and given lots of TLC! During rest times, we do play soft classical music to help them relax. Feeding times are at 8:00AM, 12:ooPM (if your dogs eat 3x/day) and 5:00PM. Please supply their food at drop off. We also have webcams around the facility so you can always check in on your pups whenever you like!

Pricing

  • Half Day (1-4 Hours): $15/dog
  • Full Day (5-8 Hours): $30/dog
  • *If needed, medication administration is included*

Boarding

Our boarding services are second to none! We have 10 climate controlled suites, and each one has a fun theme. The Scooby Suite is a client favorite! All suites have unrestricted access to individual outdoor grassy areas for unlimited potty breaks, and they're equipped with a webcam that allows you to check on your pet any time you like. During the day, your pup will join in on our daycare fun, including 2 walks/day. Feeding times are at 8:00AM, 12:ooPM (if your dogs eat 3x/day) and 5:00PM. Please supply their food at drop off. Your dog will feel like they're on vacation while you're on vacation. What more could you ask for?

Pricing

  • $50/night for the first dog
  • $40/night for each additional dog
  • *If needed, medication administration is included*

Training

We offer puppy classes, adult classes, agility classes, and AKC classes (Canine Good Citizen, Community Canine, and Urban Canine). Our classes meet once a week for six weeks. For specific class dates, please give us a call at 123-456-7891 or submit an inquiry using the form at the bottom of this page.


Pricing

  • $150 for 1 six week course

Grooming

Whether you want your pup to be fresh after their stay with us or if they just need a spa day, we've got you covered! All baths include organic shampoo, blow-dry, ear cleaning, brushing, pet friendly perfume/cologne, and a pawdicure (no polish). Please note, grooming prices can vary based on your dog's size and hair length. Feel free to stop by for a consultation!

Pricing

  • Bath: $30
  • Bath & De-Shed Treatment: $45
  • Bath & Hair Cut: $45
  • Bath, De-Shed & Hair Cut: $80
  • Nail Trim & Buff: $12
